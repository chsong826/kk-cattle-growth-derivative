🐂 Kedah-Kelantan Cattle Study & Data Origin

This simulation is calibrated using growth performance data collected from indigenous Kedah-Kelantan cattle reared under tropical semi-intensive grazing systems in Kelantan and Kedah, Malaysia.

The function f(x) calculates cumulative body weight at age x. The derivative f'(x) gives the exact instantaneous daily gain.
