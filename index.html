🐂 Kedah-Kelantan Cattle Study & Data Origin

This simulation is calibrated using performance data from Kedah-Kelantan (KK) cattle, one of Malaysia’s indigenous beef cattle breeds. The cattle were reared under semi-intensive tropical grazing systems in Kelantan and Kedah, Malaysia.

The function f(x) calculates cumulative body weight at age x. The derivative f'(x) represents the instantaneous daily weight gain of the cattle.

Cattle Profile Summary

  • • Breed: Kedah-Kelantan (KK) Cattle
  • • Production Type: Tropical Beef Cattle
  • • Housing System: Semi-Intensive Grazing
  • • Location: Kelantan & Kedah, Malaysia
  • • Standard Growth Cycle: 24 Months
